About Us

Our Mission

We are a youth-led AI EdTech project dedicated to providing high-quality educational resources to girls in Afghanistan. Using interactive video lessons, AI-powered learning tools, and personalized assignments, we aim to make learning accessible, engaging, and impactful.

We strive to empower students through technology, inspire lifelong learning, and create opportunities for girls to reach their full potential.

We welcome collaboration with partners, NGOs, and investors to expand our reach and impact. If you are a student, educator, or supporter, you can join us and access our programs by submitting an application.

The problems we solve

Many girls in Afghanistan face barriers to quality education due to limited access, social restrictions, and lack of interactive learning tools. We aim to bridge this gap by offering video lessons, interactive exercises, and educational games that students can access safely from home.

Why AI

Artificial Intelligence allows us to personalize learning for each student, track progress, provide instant feedback, and create engaging, adaptive educational experiences. AI helps us scale our impact efficiently, reaching more students with high-quality learning tailored to their needs.

How It Works

  1. Content Creation

    Teachers record video lessons across main subjects.

  2. AI Support

    Students receive explanations, practice tasks, and personalized feedback through AI tools.

  3. Secure Access

    Materials are delivered safely to girls and women in restricted regions.

  4. Certification

    After completing modules, learners earn digital certificates.

HerAccess combines human teaching and AI technology to provide real education in difficult conditions.
